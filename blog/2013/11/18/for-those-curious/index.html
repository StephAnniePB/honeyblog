For Those Curious

Nov 18th, 2013 10:20 pm

When the Lord decides no more yarn is needed for the tapestry of my life
I pray I am sleeping held in the arms of a man
Who is aware of my faults yet loves me anyway…
I pray that the Lord has answered
My plea to make me better by blessing me with babies
Healthy strong beautiful children
Who won’t bear my burdens but will rise because of me

Before my body succumbs to a spirit stronger than itself
I pray that in my classroom I spark the imagination
 Of one who will change the world
Not by becoming rich and famous
But by reaching out helping others to achieve their dreams

Cerebral Palsy can’t hold me alive
and the ground won’t hold me when I’m gone
Cremate me allow fire to lick my body turning me to ash
And sprinkle me off the Georgia Coast
Allowing me to be free at last

Don’t cry for me I cried enough tears while living for those who mourn
And whatever you do don’t allow people to spin rosy pictures of the life to which I was born

Tell the truth
Never forget that beside the word stubborn is a picture of me
Remember my laughter and my faith but
If you want to see and know my heart read my poetry


On the night you follow these instructions throw a party
And I’m not talking one of those quiet affairs
I mean a full-scale ani’t nobody leaving ‘til the music stops joints
I want men and ladies dressed to the nines as you raise a glass to me with everybody focused on having a good time
For those curious this is how I want my journey to end
And why I’m determined not to let you turn me ‘round before it begins.
