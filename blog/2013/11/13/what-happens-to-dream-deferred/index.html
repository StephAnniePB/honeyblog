What Happens to a Dream Deferred?

Nov 13th, 2013 9:29 am

What happens to a dream deferred?
Langston’s voice still resonates
His question lingers unanswered
Holding vigil at the gates


What happens to a dream deferred?
For him it festered like a sore 
For me its oozed and puckered
Thick with anger resentment and more

What happens to a dream deferred?
It inhales your self-esteem.
