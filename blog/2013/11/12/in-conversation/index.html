In Conversation

Nov 12th, 2013 8:25 am

Intriguing Intelligence
Immediately Imploring Me
To ignore the physical and concentrate
Concentrate ‘cuz
Pure Possibility radiates between you and me
Hissing and snapping
Scenting the air
 An Alpha’s Awareness held in your speech
Neither a smooth baritone nor a clear tenor
It seeps into my bones as I struggle to define the tone
Watching strong lips move
In Conversation

In Conversation you proved powerful
Holding your own in debate
Challenging me to conversate

Unfortunately, this life isn’t lived in conversation
