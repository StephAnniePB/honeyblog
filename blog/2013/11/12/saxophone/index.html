Saxophone

Nov 12th, 2013 8:22 am

Sweet n’ gentle
Smooth n’ sassy
Hot n’ spicy
This is the instrument
I was born to play
Rhythms come
Rich n’ raw
Like the blood through my veins
This is real
The notes are mine
All mine
I play best all alone
When New Orleans sleeps
And only Louis watches as I creep
Many people say
I should go to Juilliard
But what they can’t see
It’s a gift not for exploitation
I play sweet n’ sour
Misery n’ pain
I play the heart of New Orleans
Every note has its value
I do this for enjoyment
To remember
To escape
To relax
Relaxin’
Most people don’t
They’s too busy stressin’
They should let the
Sax teach its lesson
Its smooth like that
Cool like that
Real like that
Plays for me
R&B
Jazz
Even things that have no name.
