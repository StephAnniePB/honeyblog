I Love You...Me

Nov 20th, 2013 6:10 pm

My ambition cracks the sky
My spirit flashes illuminating darkness
I Shed ice crystals or  balmy bath water Tears
I am the hurricane crashing to shore
To the naked eye I am a placid lake 
my persistence weakens rock, my anger tears through buildings
leaving kitchens standing
kitchens are my heart
Stores of Kindness,
Caring coupled with craftiness upon Pantry Shelves
I relish re-mixing bread, Cheese and Dough
Few taste it though
My movements recall the thump of bass, the kick of the snare covers me
I am what people drive miles to see.

My Passion ignites the sun
Few Step Towards it free of fear
I am Jubilance Jumping Joyously
Sparking those within my Atmosphere
My Integrity is rooted like the Peach tree
Generations Gain Guidance, tinged with southern Hospitality
I am the Blues At Sunrise, The Blues on Sunday, Despite what others say
I maintain the Right to trouble Trouble and Always Misbehave

The Warmth of other Suns intrigues me
 Curiosity Constantly Calls
And God Sits Above It All

“Stephanie, I Love You… Me.”
