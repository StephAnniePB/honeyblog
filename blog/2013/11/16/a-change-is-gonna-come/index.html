A Change Is Gonna Come

Nov 16th, 2013 7:33 pm

Mama & Daddy, after a hard day
Us kids, an’ things not havin’ gone their way
Would put us down for the night
Makin’ sure we said our prayers an’ turned on night lights
Before creepin’ downstairs to hold each other tight

Daddy would give her a certain glance  Mama knew without askin’
He wanted a dance
The record spun an’ they’d be lost
Returning to where their love begun

One night I snuck downstairs
After a fright before sayin’ my prayers

Eyes saucer wide I leaned into the door
Mama held Daddy as he cried
I slid to the floor

First Medgar and Malcolm; Now Martin
Lord; Why?
How many more men have to die?

It was a whispered plea
Yet their pain even the blind could see

I slipped into the room
Going for the records behind the broom
One luxury we could afford

I handled them with care
Praying Sam Cooke would be there

I asked Mama why they liked him so
She said I’d learn as I go
They left me not too long ago
Memories fill the silence though

An’ still after a hard day
Work
An’ things not going my way

I travel back to then
As I walk through my den
Slippin’ quietly in the room to the CD’s
Underneath my T.V.

My whole body begins to hum
Sam says
My Change is Gonna Come
