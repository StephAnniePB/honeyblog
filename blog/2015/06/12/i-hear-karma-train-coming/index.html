I Hear the Karma Train Coming..........

Jun 12th, 2015 5:30 pm

I thought of you as awake; someone I could accept Master Card from until I realized you did not want to be Master nor Commander of your fate.
I thought of you as awake; someone I could tear down the walls for until I realized you were a mere mirage; a fake.
I thought of you as awake; then I saw you would rather steal from me than build with me.
I hear the Karma Train Coming...... moving stealthily unhurried by your arrogance.
