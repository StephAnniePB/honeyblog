I Came to Love

May 31st, 2015 6:43 pm

I came to love; came to the acceptance and freedom tucked inside home.
I came to love; came to soak in the warmth of those who don't share my blood yet hear my heart.
I came to love; came to hear her cadence her rhythm granting permission for mine to become softer slowly recalling southern roots.
I came to love; came to watch my nephews curiosity as they explore.
I came to love; came to hear his wisdom for further growing my faith as I continue my journey.
I came to love; came here in a community struggling to stand I came to love.
