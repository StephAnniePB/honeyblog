--- layout: post title: "Ancestors Answered" date: 2014-01-12T12:24:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
Ancestors answered. Their words rooted in me. Food for when I have none. Fed from the fruits of their spirits. Led by voices that whisper. Acknowledging I must be still to hear it. 
Ancestors answered. Hours spent digesting their scrolls. Turning thoughts into action with pen strokes across life's page. Ancestors answered as I stand in the gap. Nourished from their sorrows and strengthened by their triumphs. On their shoulders I stand.
