--- layout: post title: "The Moment the Truth Doesn't Answer the Questions" date: 2015-01-24T19:24:00-06:00 ---
Anyone who knows me knows I have struggled to answer one question my entire life; "Who am I?" Growing up in two different foster care systems in two different states has left that question echoing in my brain for many years. Tonight I took a gaint step and did a background check on a former foster parent. However after I saw the information I realized that it was only a small sliver of the puzzle and now I find myself at the moment when the truth doesn't answer the questions. The journey continues...............
