If ever I doubt Love
 I REMEMBER Sunday Breakfast
Banana WALNUT WAFFLES
Peach Pink Melon Cubes
Pressed Meyer Lemons
Lounging in A sugar Bath
Bass pumping through tile
 Fire Wrangled
Bacon basking on the stove
Awesome Aromas awaken me
Feet fleecing stairs
Catching kitchen’s call
I instinctively inquire
Knowing you’ve done it All.
Willfully waiting when walking whispered…….
You still stayed standing
Spatula in hand
Flipping flapjacks, FlapJacks Flipping
If ever I doubt love my mind returns To You
Sunday Breakfast being made.
