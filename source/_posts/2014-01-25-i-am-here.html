--- layout: post title: "I Am Here." date: 2014-01-25T19:43:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
I am a survivor because I am here.
I am here refusing to shatter
Standing instead of splintering from the impact of abuse.

I am a warrior because I am here.
I am here refusing to break under the cruelty of your tongue.
I will let Karma deal with what you have done.

I will not take an eye for an eye and leave us both blind.
I will respect your humanity if you respect mine.

I am one who thrives because I am here.
I am here helping others; being a sister, a friend, a confidante,  a lover.
I thrive because I can forgive another.

The voice of your soul was strangled by the things you wished to own.
 Little did you know I was eager to give you a home.

I am a survivor because I am here.
