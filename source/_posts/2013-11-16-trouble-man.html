--- layout: post title: "Trouble Man " date: 2013-11-16T19:37:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
You were an intriguing enigma
Right from the start
The Protector
The Charmer
The Racketeer
Better than an actor preparing parts

My personal private magician
Conjuring up the you
I needed you to be
My Client
My Friend
My Lover
Was it mere tricks of light
And sleight of hand

But this isn’t all on you
I troubled trouble
I kissed
I undressed
I laid down
And Lord knows
It still takes two

When she’s old enough
To talk things through
And ask tough questions
Of Me and You

With heavy sighs
Shall come this reply

It was only meant as
A one-night stand
We troubled trouble
You understand
