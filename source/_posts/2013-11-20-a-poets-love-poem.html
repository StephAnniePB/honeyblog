--- layout: post title: "A Poet's Love Poem " date: 2013-11-20T18:08:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
Nas only needs one mic
To entice you to purchase
His words on wax
And though you’re a skeptic
I only need my pen
To draw you in
With mere pen strokes
I can reshape ordinary things
Make you blush
At the thought of water
And the pleasures it brings
Without leaving my desk
I can explore your contours
Let my eyes inhale you
As my lips moan in praise
Of your brown body
And the way you’ve matured
I’ll make you a metaphor
Wrap you in rhythm and rhyme
All for the pleasure
Of some quality time
I’ll indulge alliteration
Stroking softly slowly
Up your spine
For the promise
You’ll do the same to mine.
