Day 7: Sunday August 31st 

  1. Thankful for hip hop because it gave me a home when I lost mine, because it let me know that I was not alone. 
  2. Thankful for warmth and sunshine.
  3. Thankful for the healing power of a hot shower.
  4. Thankful for the ability to break bread with a loved one.
  5. Thankful for Sunday; my favorite day.
  6. Thankful for calmness.
  7. Thankful for time to continue my memoir.
  8. Thankful for the desire to learn.
  9. Thankful to be able to manifest my desire to learn into things that help my community.
The goal today is not to force things. As pressed as I am to make certain changes I know that they will not come through force. I must trust that the doors will open at the appointed time.
