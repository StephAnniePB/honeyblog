Today, we lost a Poet. This truth teller told us to unplug from the Matrix long before Hollywood.
He put pen to paper to tell us Black is beautiful. Black is bold. Black is resistance. Black is fearless. Black is revolution.
Today we lost a Poet. A prophet who like so many was silenced because he refused the status quo.  Ancestor those of us who know you stood in the gap for us say thank you. Thank you for being fearlessly strong. May the ancestors welcome you home.
Today, we lost a Poet.
