Ladies Listen
Now, if your man, is your most prized possession
Then these few verses are not for you
All Others Pay Attention
If you find
The brother strong enough
To look you in the eye
Recognizing your woman’s worth
And your potential
To do more than just survive
Here’s what I want y’all to do for him
When he calls you after
A long ass day of walking around securitizing
Asking for a hot meal
Laced with a little something
He can feel
Slowly say yes
Remember
He could be running the streets
Catching cases
Adding to his felony
Putting his piece in people’s faces
And you could be
Home alone with your heart in your throat
Every time the newscaster speaks
Praying His isn’t the murder they wrote

Didn’t think of that, did you?

This next verse I shouldn’t even have to say
But open your ears ladies
And listen anyway
If you find
The brother wise enough
To blaze his own path
Toward his unalienable rights
Life, Liberty, Love and Happiness
Here’s what I want y’all to do for him
When he conversates with you
Using words to paint
His dreams in every vivid hue
Your actions will speak louder than words
Show him you believe in the possibilities
Because
He could be on the corner
Talkin’ ‘bout
I see no reason to move
They’re the ones who owe me
Forty acres and a mule
And you could have a front row seat
Watching his talent and purpose drip
Melted ice cream in Midsummer heat

Food for thought, isn’t it?

Follow Instructions… Please… Listen…
If you find
The brother perceptive enough
To invite you into his comfort zone
A man whose purest pleasure
Comes from hearing you moan
Here’s what I want y’all to do for him
Respect yourself
This part of your relationship
Is as sacred as life itself
Remember this
When runnin’ with your crew
And another asks
Can I leave my signature with you
He could be accepting offers
From the girls in suburbia
Circling like vultures
At the sight of fresh meat
And you could be attacking his privacy
Going through pockets
Scrolling through messages on his two way
Jealousy turns brown eyes green
Her soft-spoken words turn to screams

 Do Right Woman, Do Right Man

Joy is free, ladies, but happiness has a price
If you find
The brother humble enough
To accept this
A man secure enough
To realize his footsteps are the Lord’s to control
Here’s what I want y’all to do for him
Give the Lord your emotional baggage
Ask the Heavenly Father
To open your heart’s eyes
Allowing you true access
To the man at your side
Never forget
We Women may have fought
For our right to roar
But God took Adam’s rib
To enable Eve’s footsteps
Across the Garden floor
Just think
Your man could believe
He’s in control
And you could be
With your Lord
Asking him to show mercy
For the man whose soul was left behind
