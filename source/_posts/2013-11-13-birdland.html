--- layout: post title: "Birdland " date: 2013-11-13T09:34:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---


Chetlooks as if the pole to Birdland is holding him up
As if flying on blue notes
Has taken his energy
Four in the morning; Hollow cheeked; Blurry eyed
The cigarette burns unattended in his right hand
Maybe tonight he’ll go home and leave the needles alone
He can hear the noise of people milling about
The lights from Lindy’s and the Broadway Sports Center bring premature daylight
He stands oblivious to it all and Clax snaps the picture
Freezing…

Birdland 4a.m. 1960 forever.
