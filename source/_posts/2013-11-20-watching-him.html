--- layout: post title: "Watching Him" date: 2013-11-20T17:59:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
The assignment is to develop a character 
Someone so real they jump off the page 
Everyone else is doing it 
I hear the clicking of Word keys 
And yet I wonder at his age



I wish I had the guts to ask 
Is your scent soap and water alone?
Teachers glancing right at me
Oh…the assignment 
Guess I’d better finish my task




Just one more thing 
Do you mind if I relax and stare?
