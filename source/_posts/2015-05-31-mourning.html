--- layout: post title: "Mourning " date: 2015-05-31T22:49:00-05:00 categories: - Poems ---
Strange; the luxury of silence amid the commotion of grief. That moment you slip among the trees to tell them your troubles; feeling normal is just around the corner yet unable to find its rhythm where you were last. I want to wake up and hear your laugh, see your smile or hear you tell me one more time to be myself. I want your wisdom and love bottled.
