--- layout: post title: "Organically Grown Love " date: 2014-09-01T05:55:00-05:00 categories: - Poems ---
Fertilize tiny seeds of conversation
Ferment ideas with hopes and dreams
Seek what lies below the A-horizon
Plumb the depths of my mind with inquiries
Ask what countries I want to see; what languages I speak
Challenge me to continue becoming my highest self.

When you reach the B-horizon
Fertilize me with bone meal
Seek to speak my love language
Learn why Sundays are my favorite
Know that what I ask of you I ask of myself first

At the C-horizon
Become my partner in peace
Let us agree to never take things personally
Let us speak with integrity
In our ecosystem our word is bond

Let us create an environment where kindness and compassion
cross-pollinate with strength and ingenuity
Where seeds of knowledge become sprouts of wisdom harvested at the appointed time
Where the branches of our family tree drop fruits of the spirit in June

Imagine answering the ancestors call
Let us rebuild
My heart swears we can have it all
