My mind maneuvers mine fields
War this warrior does not want 
Yet peace and purpose are not kin 

On destinations do not dwell 
Choose your own adventure
Choose combinations that nourish 
Know thy darkness to bring forth thy light
Know thy weaknesses to bring forth thy strength

My mind maneuvers mine fields 
War this warrior does not want 
Yet liberty and freedom are not kin
