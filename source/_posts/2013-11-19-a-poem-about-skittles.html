--- layout: post title: "A Poem About Skittles" date: 2013-11-19T21:42:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
I heard a poem about Skittles once and all I could think was Emmett
And how Nikki said he made it cross country because of the Pullman Porters
Now Dr. Phil is in the mix trying to convince Trayvon’s parents
They have the right to grieve
Through tears I wonder why he thinks he has the right
Wonder when we will understand
No one has the right to disable your feelings
Turn them into sound bites
For ratings
I heard a poem about Skittles once and all I could think was Day La  Beckworth hid in bushes
To take a father from a son
He said “Killing that nigger gave me no more discomfort than our wives at child birth.”
His laughter crawls across the sky taking root
Strange fruit……..
Billie’s voice pulls pain pulsing a bitterness hidden in yes’mam

I heard a poem about skittles once.
