--- layout: post title: "Just Thinking" date: 2013-11-17T18:20:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
Just thinking… Just cogitating…
Will you read the whole book, soak up every word, savoring silken syllables
Mining it for treasures trouble hasn't taken
Or is this just a courtesy look?
Just evaluating….
You were bold enough to ante-up but are you strong enough to stay
Table stakes are high here physical chemistry will never replace mental intimacy
Just deliberating…
We aren’t two of a kind but your hustle matches mine
Just ruminating …
Wondering if you understand money might be this worlds darling
 But affection flames passion; passion births babies; babies become children changing galaxies
Just …
Wondering if you want me like I want you
Just musing…
Can I write a good breakfast and cook a great poem…
I think… I thought if I threw down in the kitchen you’d know I’m hugging you from the inside out
Trying to figure what real relations are about
Just pondering…sauntering through thoughts like

What happens if I unwrap wounds of neglect would it affect your bet?
