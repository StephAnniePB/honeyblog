--- layout: post title: "A Poet's Dilemma " date: 2013-11-13T09:26:00-06:00 categories: - Poems ---
Lord,
Ken has asked me 
To think twice of a chance to change it all
And so it is on You 
I’ve come to call
Though I’ve seen my share of pain
You’ve Blessed me 
With steady strength to get through rain
Though my eyes may glow 
At the chance to choose, 
What strikes me most
Are the things I’d lose 


Let’s say you decided 
To change a life 
Especially Mine 
What would become then 
Of my favorite pastime 
Would I still 
Hold such a powerful pen



And what of this rich imagination
Would I still 
Have this sharp mind 
With which to hold 
My own in conversation 



I can’t possibly Fathom 
Not being able to read
Or smile as my muse 
Plants his fresh seed




My life would be absurd 

Without taking time
To eat the honey of words 
Guess I’ll have 
To stay me then
Hope that’s all right with You and Ken
